Company Vision

As San Clan Ent, our vision is to be known as a professional and socially sustainable society. Who takes it upon themselves to make a positive difference in society. We would like to be known as a reliable and social society that organises, and produces performances of the greatest quality. We would also like to be known as a society that aims to make a difference in society.

That being said, our vision is to be the prime entertainment society that prides itself on professionalism, authenticity and transparency through the delivery of unique and quality services and products with variety in entertainment that complements the lifestyle of our customers and employees. We also believe in the act of humanitarian, as we aim to make a positive impact on society and the environment as a whole as action speaks louder than words.

Company Mission

As San Clan Ent, our mission is to promote a healthy and active lifestyle through our music, initiatives and most importantly the culture. We aim to create awareness and educate society on the benefits of what we have to offer. Provide a safe and friendly environment for audiences at our outings, events and service projects. Promote San Clan Ent, and any other initiative by wearing the branded attire, and sharing on social media about our experiences, as well as what they offer.

We also aim to achieve the company’s goals and objectives in managing the projects we are affiliated in, our missions are as follows:

  • Develop services, products and entertainment that complement and fulfil the ever-changing needs of the customers.
  • Focus on developing systems that improve the quality of human resource management. Putting in place mechanisms that encourage, create and develop people are knowledgeable, skillful and have the emotional intelligence to question, accept and improve our services and products.
  • Operate the business in compliance with ethical corporate governance.

Company Values

As San Clan Ent, our values are not only music related. The values we pride ourselves on, are core to why we do what we do. Our core values are as follows:

  • A belief in being a good steward of resources and in exercising frugality.
  • A belief that humanity is of fundamental importance.
  • A belief in maintaining a healthy work/life balance.
  • A commitment to sustainability and acting in an environmentally friendly way.
  • A commitment to using music as a catalyst for change.

As San Clan Ent, we also have some guiding questions that we also refer to, as a reminder. Those questions allow us to move into a higher level of thinking, by providing a more open-ended support that turns our attention towards key details without being perspective. Those questions are, “How can my actions eventually change the world”, “How can we ensure that conflicts lead to constructive change and positive outcomes for everyone involved”, and “How can we create, test and validate a model that will benefit society, in more ways than one."
